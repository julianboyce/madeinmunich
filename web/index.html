WHO ARE WE?

Munich Made is a loose collective of coders, designers and entrepeneurs that start businesses and make great software. We’re coming to you from beautiful Bavaria in the crotchety but charming city of Munich.

WHAT DO WE DO?

We’re collecting and sharing great products and businesses that make their home here in Bavaria. We want to build up the community, form connections, and get in touch with other creatives.

HOW DO I JOIN?

Just stick a badge on your site and send us an email letting us know why you’re a fit. The more the merrier.
